About NepalEdu Map

NepalEdu Map is a platform dedicated to identifying and mapping unreached schools across Nepal. By visualizing the educational landscape, we aim to bring awareness and resources to schools in need.

Explore the Map

Use this map to locate schools and their current status. Help us identify unreached schools by contributing data.

How to Contribute

You can help us by:

  • Submitting data about schools in your area.
  • Volunteering for field mapping projects.
  • Donating resources to support schools.
